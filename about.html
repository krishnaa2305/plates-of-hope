About Plates Of Hope

Bridging the gap between food waste and hunger by connecting generous donors with those in need

Our Mission

At Plates Of Hope, we believe that no one should go hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our mission is to create a seamless platform where restaurants, food businesses, and generous individuals can donate their excessive leftover food to us and then we provide that food to our registered ngo.

We're building a world where surplus food finds its way to empty plates, creating a sustainable cycle of giving that benefits everyone involved.

What We Do

We've created a comprehensive ecosystem that makes food donation simple, safe, and impactful

Restaurant Partnerships

We partner with restaurants, cafes, and food businesses to rescue surplus food that would otherwise go to waste. Our platform makes it easy for establishments to donate excess inventory at the end of each day.

Individual Contributions

Community members can easily donate homemade meals, groceries, through our platform. We provide guidelines to ensure all donations meet our safety guidelines.

Smart Distribution

Our network of volunteers and partner organizations ensures donated food reaches those who need it most. We use technology to optimize routes and minimize food spoilage.

Our Story

Plates Of Hope was born from a simple observation: while millions of people face food insecurity, tons of perfectly good food is wasted daily. Founded in 2025 by a team of 3 students.

What started as a small local initiative now we are in 3 cities in Delhi Ncr and planning for expending to more cities soon. We've facilitated over 1000 meals and prevented two hundred kilogram of food from ending up in landfills.
